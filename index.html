My favorite place to get food is Chipotle

There is a Walmart, GameStop, and Brewtop Bar right next to Chipotle.

Directions to Chipotle from KCI

Kansas City International Airport

  1. Head South on i29
  2. Take exit 8
  3. Take a left onto barry road
  4. Take another left onto Ambassador Drive
  5. Chipotle will be on your left
